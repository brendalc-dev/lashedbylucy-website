About Lash Extensions

Lash extensions are a semi-permanent solution for enhancing the length, curl, fullness, and thickness of your natural lashes. The process involves applying individual synthetic lashes to your natural lashes using a semi-permanent adhesive. This procedure results in longer, fuller lashes that can last several weeks with proper care. The application time typically ranges from 2 to 3 hours, depending on the desired fullness, and allows for lash wrapping, which helps to improve retention and durability.

The Lash Extension Application Process:

  1. Consultation: The technician discusses your lash goals, checks for any allergies, and recommends the best lash style (classic, volume, hybrid, etc.) based on your natural lashes.
  2. Preparation: Your eyes are cleaned to remove makeup or oils, and a gel pad or tape is applied to secure the lower lashes to protect the under-eye area.
  3. Lash Selection: The technician selects extensions based on your desired style and lash length. The extensions are chosen to complement your natural lashes for a balanced, natural look.
  4. Application: Using specialized tweezers, the technician applies a synthetic lash extension to each individual natural lash with a small amount of adhesive. Lash wrapping may be used to improve retention.
  5. Drying and Setting: The adhesive is allowed to dry and set, ensuring a strong and long-lasting bond between the natural lashes and extensions.
  6. Aftercare: Once the extensions are applied, the technician will provide aftercare instructions to maintain your lashes and prevent early shedding.

With proper aftercare, lash extensions can last up to 3-4 weeks, after which a touch-up is recommended to maintain the fullness and shape.
